# VEX ROBOTICS Screw Sorter
This is an automatic screw sorter, designed for standard VEX screws of sizes 0.25" up to 2.00". 
Other screws such as shoulder screws may not work.

THis design crucially **doesn't require a human operator** to manaully place screws on the sorter - just dump your screws in the box, turn the switch on, and get it sorted automatically.
