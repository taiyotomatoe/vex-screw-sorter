# Automatic Screw Sorter for VEX Robotics
**An automatic screw sorter, designed for standard VEX screws of sizes 0.25" up to 2.00".**  
*Note: Other screws such as shoulder screws may not work.*

We all hate having to sort our screws. 
That's why this screw sorter works **without requiring a human operator** to manually place screws on the sorter - just dump your screws in the box, start the motor, and get it sorted automatically.
